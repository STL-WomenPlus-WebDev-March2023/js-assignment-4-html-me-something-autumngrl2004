My Favorite Artist!

Music has always been a big part of my life. Music has comforted me, motivated me, excited me, always keeps me singing like a fool in my car, and has played a part in so many bonds and memories. Everyone has those Artists that stick around through time and speak to their soul, heres my favorite.

#1 Ryan Caraveo

The #1 spot goes to Northwest's finest, Ryan Caraveo. Ryan Caraveo has many different sounds. His more recent albums consist of an alternative / pop sound, while his older songs are hip hop. I can't say he has any songs that i truly dislike, but I love almost all of them. Along with thinking he creates catchy music that I can vibe with; I think he is a talented lyricist as well.


A 30 year old melodic rapper from seattle, most of his older songs have to do with his drive to succeed with the cards he was dealt in life, dealing with and overcoming depression, relationship issues, and a feeling of always needing to be better stemmed from not feeling content with himself. I started listening to him and immediately related to alot of the details he put in his songs and started listening to more. I can appreciate alot of different genres, but I usually get hooked to a song because of the words and instruments. I think I naturally appreciate songs more when the artists delicatelly put the words in place because I have such hard time doing so and really feeling like i'm saying what I really wanted to put out there. Through the years, like every artist, their style changes with their growth (not always for the better), and so did his. His music mainly changed to a more pop/ electronic and melodic sound, mainly talking about not being in the same mental place he was, some relationship songs, and a cover of Avril Lavigne's "Complicated" w/ DJ Aoki that's not the same as the original but definitely a great upbeat version.
