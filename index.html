My Favorite Artist!

Music has always been a big part of my life. Music has comforted me, motivated me, excited me, always keeps me singing like a fool in my car, and has played a part in so many bonds and memories. Everyone has those Artists that stick around through time and speak to their soul, heres my favorite.

#1 Ryan Caraveo

The #1 spot goes to Northwest's finest, Ryan Caraveo. Ryan Caraveo has many different sounds. His more recent albums consist of an alternative / pop sound, while his older songs are hip hop. I can't say he has any songs that i truly dislike, but I love almost all of them. Along with thinking he creates catchy music that I can vibe with; I think he is a talented lyricist and I can truly relate to his lyrics.
